Foster Farms Wings in Air Fryer

Description

Instructions for how to cook Foster Farms frozen wings in an air fryer

Prep: 0 min

Cook time: 20 mins

Makes: As mucha as wanted

Ingredients

  • Foster Farms Crispy Take Out Wings

Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 360 degrees
  2. Cook for 12 minutes
  3. Flip/shake wings in basket
  4. Place back into air fryer and cook at 400 degrees for 6 minutes
  5. Toss in sauce of choosing
