Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description

Delicious muffin recipe created by my own grandmother

Prep Time: 30 min

Bake Time: 25 min

Makes: 2 dozen

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/3 cups mashed ripe bananas (about 3 bananas)
  • 1 cup butter/margarine
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate pieces
